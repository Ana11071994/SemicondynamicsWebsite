Learn the dynamics of Semiconductor Ecosystem

The workshop offers a comprehensive understanding of the semiconductor ecosystem, including its components, trends, supply chain dynamics, market forces, and collaboration opportunities. It uses lectures, case studies, interactive discussions, and hands-on activities to engage participants and equip them with practical knowledge.
